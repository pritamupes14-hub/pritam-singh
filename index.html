01. About Me

With 10+ years of experience advising Fortune 500 companies and National Oil Companies (NOCs) on their business & knowledge needs, I specialize in the formulation and implementation of both conventional and energy transition strategies.

In an era of market volatility, my work is defined by trying to solve directly with the clients – bridging the gap between legacy operational demands and the urgent need for achieving the Energy Quadrilemma: Security, Affordability, Sustainability, all while ensuring long-term Profitability.

I translate complex market signals into actionable roadmaps through:

  • Strategy Enablement: Opportunity identification, regulatory intelligence, business development, so-what analysis, value-chain mapping, and go-to-market (GTM) execution.
  • Market Intelligence: Deep-dive market & competitive analysis (MICI) and investment decision support.
  • Diverse Energy Portfolio: Expertise across Oil & Gas, LNG, Hydrogen, Ammonia, Renewables, and the Circular Economy sectors.

Key Highlights:

  • 🤝 C-Suite Advisory: Partnered with CXOs to validate business models and stress-test strategies against global market volatility and regulatory disruption.
  • 🛠️ Solution & Capability Architecture: Developed proprietary capability decks and strategic frameworks, supported by multiple industry publications on key themes.
  • 📈 Market Entry & Expansion: Spearheaded several $1 Mn+ business accounts of conventional and clean energy firms, translating complex market signals into actionable growth.
  • 🧪 Hydrogen & Low-Carbon Growth: Designed business frameworks for low-carbon fuel adoption and large-scale hydrogen infrastructure deployment.
  • 👥 Cross-Functional Leadership: Orchestrated multi-disciplinary teams to deliver cohesive, large-scale strategies for global energy players.

🔗 Open to: Strategy, Market Intelligence, Advisory, and Consulting roles in Energy & Clean Energy space.
